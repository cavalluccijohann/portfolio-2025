Hello, my name is Johann, and I am a software engineer based in Paris, France.

I am passionate about creation, mainly software and website development. I have always loved creating. From a young age, I would make all sorts of gadgets… and for the past 5 years, I have channeled this passion into development and design. I love imagining a project and bringing it to life by experimenting with and testing new technologies. I have learned to share this art by collaborating within a team.

I would describe my professional approach as an insatiable desire to learn by constantly taking on new challenges. I sincerely believe that every day is an opportunity to improve and become the person you aspire to be.
